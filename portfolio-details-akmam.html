AKmam – Full-Stack Charity & Nonprofit Platform

A fully functional, admin-managed charity platform built with React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB. Designed to empower nonprofits, it features donation processing, volunteer registration, project showcases, and a blog for storytelling. With seven dynamic pages (Home, About, Donate, Volunteer, Projects, Blog, Contact), AKmam helps organizations promote their mission, raise funds, and connect with supporters worldwide.
